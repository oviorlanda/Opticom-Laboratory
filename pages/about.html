Opticom Laboratory is a laboratory that operates in the field of transmission, specifically in the field of fiber optics.

About Opticom Laboratory

Optical Communication System Laboratory (Opticomm) is one of the transmission laboratories at Telkom University which is engaged in optical communication technology. The Opticomm Laboratory was inaugurated in 1999, and has main activity in terms of organizing practicum for the Optical Communication System course for bachelor students of Telecommunication Engineering at Telkom University.

In addition to practical activities, we also conduct research that focuses on optical wireless communication, such as: Visible Light Communication, Vehicle to Vehicle Communication, Underwater Visible Light Communication, Hybrid Radio Frequency-Visible Light Communication, Free Space Optic, Underground Mining Visible Light Communication, and many more.

Visi

Ours Visi

To become an internationally accredited Telecommunications Engineering undergraduate program actively involved in the development of education, research, and entrepreneurship in the field of digital telecommunications engineering.

Mission

Ours Missions

  • To provide an excellent and internationally accredited education program that includes and accommodates entrepreneurship initiatives.
  • To develop and disseminate telecommunications science and technology, especially in wireless communication, recognized globally.
  • To organize entrepreneurship programs among the academic community, focusing on technology-based telecommunications innovations.
  • To apply telecommunications science and technology for the betterment of national civilization and the global community.
