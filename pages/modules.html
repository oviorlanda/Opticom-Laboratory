Module 01

Fiber Optic Fundamentals

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of fiber optic communication systems. Students will learn about the basic principles of light propagation in optical fibers, types of optical fibers, and their characteristics.

The module covers essential topics including total internal reflection, numerical aperture, modes of propagation, dispersion, and attenuation in optical fibers. Students will gain hands-on experience with fiber optic components and basic measurement techniques.

Module 02

Optical Transmission Systems

This module focuses on optical transmission systems and network components. Students will explore the architecture of optical communication networks, including transmitters, receivers, and optical amplifiers.

Key topics include wavelength division multiplexing (WDM), optical sources such as lasers and LEDs, photodetectors, and optical amplification technologies. The module emphasizes practical applications in modern telecommunication networks.

Module 03

Advanced Optical Communication

This advanced module covers cutting-edge topics in optical communication including free space optics, visible light communication (VLC), and emerging wireless optical technologies.

Students will explore research areas such as vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) optical communication, underwater VLC, hybrid RF-VLC systems, and optical wireless communication in challenging environments like underground mining.
